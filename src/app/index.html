About WeRemain

Our Mission

Given the threats we anticipate to marginalized communities over the next four years, our mission is to support and resource movements that are fighting to advance progress, and to protect the rights of all people in our country.

 

Who We Are

We are a group of volunteers who have created a small-dollar fundraising campaign to resource progressive groups. We will also use our network to build long-term progressive power by engaging our donors with updates on legislation and opportunities to volunteer.

 

We anticipate that organizations and movements will need additional funding to protect and defend the lives and rights of documented and undocumented immigrants, women, LGBTQ+ people, and our environment, through direct work and educational (i.e., know-your-rights) campaigns, as well as through direct action (i.e., protest/lobbying).

 

We also anticipate that groups will need volunteers. We hope to provide both funding and people – by creating a group that will sustainably help resource these organizations and by creating a network of progressive leaders from across the country.

WE'D LOVE TO HEAR FROM YOU!

 

Get in touch with WeRemain! Send us your questions, comments, and concerns, and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.

WeRemain is independently operated by volunteers and does not intend to imply a formal partnership with the organizations that are suggested for funding.
