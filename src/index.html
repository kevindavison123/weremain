Environmental Justice

Save Our Environment

Donald Trump once claimed that climate change is a hoax perpetrated by the Chinese. Not only is this demonstrably false, but such blatant disregard for scientific fact also threatens our environment and national security.

 

We selected Save Our Environment because of their work to “increase public awareness and activism on today’s most important environmental issues.” We were impressed with the range of their coalition, which brings together environmental groups with different theories of change, including Greenpeace and EarthJustice (which is currently defending NoDAPL protestors) as well as the Environmental Defense Fund, the Sierra Club, and the NRDC.
Click here for more information.

 

 

 

Protecting Our Democracy

Democracy Initiative

Donald Trump supports restrictive voter ID laws and once claimed, without evidence, that “millions of people” voted illegally in 2016. These thinly veiled attempts to disenfranchise voters under the guise of security undermine our electoral system and threaten our democracy.

 

We selected the Democracy Initiative because of their large, organized network that allows them to thoughtfully connect efforts to improve democracy – voting rights, getting money out of politics, and others – into a wide variety of groups. We were impressed with the range of their board, which includes heads of the NAACP, the Sierra Club, the AFL-CIO, Common Cause, and Greenpeace. They work to “integrate democracy into the work of issue based organizations.”
Click here for more information.

 

 

Reproductive Justice and Women's Health

Advocates For Youth

Donald Trump once claimed that there should be "some form of punishment" for doctors who perform, and women who receive, abortions. He has also voiced strong support for the repeal of Obamacare, which would severely hinder access to healthcare and reproductive services for millions of women, endangering their sexual health and well-being, and violating their constitutional rights.

 

We selected Advocates for Youth because of their work to “champion programs that recognize young people’s rights to honest sexual health information.” Advocates for Youth focuses on “accessible, confidential, and affordable sexual health services; and the resources and opportunities necessary to create sexual health equity for all youth.” Through youth activism, policy briefs, speak out campaigns, and lobbying efforts, Advocates for Youth invests in the power of young people to create change. As Debra Hauser, President of Advocates for Youth, said “[Youth leaders] won’t be stopped by these election results. They will be galvanized.” Click here for more information.

 

 

LGBTQ Rights

TransUnited

Mike Pence has stated that LGBTQ+ marriages and unions will bring about "societal collapse," and has consistently proposed and supported legislation that would inhibit the rights of the LGBTQ+ community. Such views and laws are detrimental to a large number of American citizens, who are crucial members of our communities.

 

We selected Trans United for their commitment to LGBTQ equality and building “the collective capacity of the trans community.” Through partnerships with transgender organizations across the country, Trans United “combines strategic planning, organizational development, and technical assistance to support these efforts at the local, state, and national level.” Click here for more information.

 

 

Muslim-American Rights

 

Council on Arab Islamic Relations

Donald Trump proposed a national ban on Muslim immigrants to the United States. This violates the spirit and sanctity of the First Amendment, contradicts our founding principles, and damages our relationships with the world.

 

The Council on Arab Islamic Relations (CAIR) is a civil rights advocacy group that works to “enhance understanding of Islam, encourage dialogue, protect civil liberties, empower American Muslims, and build coalitions that promote justice and mutual understanding.” We are excited to fundraise for CAIR as they continue to serve victims of discrimination and hate crimes, inform Americans of all faiths of their civil rights, and build understanding across people of all religions.

 

While CAIR is not part of the Action Network, we would encourage you to donate directly to them through their donate page. Click here for more information.

 

Undocumented Immigrants

United We Dream

From the beginning of his campaign, Donald Trump has actively demonized immigrants, particularly Latino immigrants, and has embraced harsh policies that would harm these communities. These policies not only endanger our global relations and our economic growth, but also threaten to tear families apart. This kind of rhetoric, which promotes hate and discrimination against immigrants, is counter to the basic principles of our nation.

 

We selected United We Dream because of its focus on ensuring support for young people registered under DACA during a Trump presidency, their work to help families facing deportation proceedings, and their focus on empowering local efforts. They are the United States’ largest immigrant youth-led organization.

 

United We Dream pushes against “inequities and obstacles faced by immigrant youth and believe[s] that by empowering immigrant youth, we can advance the cause of the entire community—justice for all immigrants.” United We Dream is the largest immigrant youth-led organization in the nation. Their strong local and grassroots network is made up of over 100,000 immigrant youth and allies and 55 affiliate organizations in 26 states.
Click here for more information.
