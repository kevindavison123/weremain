Protecting Muslim Rights

 

President Trump signed an executive order banning immigrants from seven predominantly Muslim countries. This violates the spirit and sanctity of the First Amendment, contradicts our founding principles, and damages our relationships with the world.

 

 

Council on Arab Islamic Relations

 

The Council on Arab Islamic Relations (CAIR) is a civil rights advocacy group that works to “enhance understanding of Islam, encourage dialogue, protect civil liberties, empower American Muslims, and build coalitions that promote justice and mutual understanding.” We are excited to fundraise for CAIR as they continue to serve victims of discrimination and hate crimes, inform Americans of all faiths of their civil rights, and build understanding across people of all religions.

 

While CAIR is not part of the Action Network, we would encourage you to donate directly to them through their
