Gabby Browning

Gabby is pretty new to the world of campaigning, but jumped at the chance to work with her friends and colleagues all across the country in creating We Remain.

 

Graduating from the University of Michigan in 2017, she is excited to continue to advocate for all people, for education, and for the arts and sciences as she continues to work with We Remain, and pursues a career in museum education.
