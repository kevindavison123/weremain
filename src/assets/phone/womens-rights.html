Protecting Women’s Health and Reproductive Justice

Donald Trump once claimed that there should be "some form of punishment" for doctors who perform, and women who receive, abortions. He has also voiced strong support for the repeal of Obamacare, which would severely hinder access to healthcare and reproductive services for millions of women, endangering their sexual health and well-being, and violating their constitutional rights.

 

 

Advocates For Youth

We selected Advocates for Youth because of their work to “champion programs that recognize young people’s rights to honest sexual health information.” Advocates for Youth focuses on “accessible, confidential, and affordable sexual health services; and the resources and opportunities necessary to create sexual health equity for all youth.” Through youth activism, policy briefs, speak out campaigns, and lobbying efforts, Advocates for Youth invests in the power of young people to create change. As Debra Hauser, President of Advocates for Youth, said “[Youth leaders] won’t be stopped by these election results. They will be galvanized.”
