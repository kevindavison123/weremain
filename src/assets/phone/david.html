David Earnhardt

David’s world view is informed by his upbringing in Montgomery, Alabama—a town known for being the birthplace of civil rights. Before joining We Remain, David served as an intern in the White House Office of Intergovernmental Affairs. Prior to his White House experience, he conducted policy research as an intern on the Health, Education, Labor and Pensions Committee in the United States Senate. David has written about the intersection of business and government and founded a college think tank to study how strategy and advocacy intersect to shape our social landscape. He currently works as a business development specialist for Amazon in Seattle, Washington. David holds a degree in Industrial Engineering from Auburn University where he was an Honors Scholar.
